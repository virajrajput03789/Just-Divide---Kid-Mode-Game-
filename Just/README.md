## 🧠 Educational Value: Division Made Fun
Designed for the **Kid Mode** curriculum, "Just Divide" turns abstract division into a physical, puzzle-solving adventure.

- **Master the Rules:** Divide larger numbers by smaller ones. If it's a perfect fit (no remainder), the tiles merge!
- **Strategy & Planning:** Use the **Keep** slot to save a powerful tile for later, or the **Trash** to clear a path.
- **Chained Reactions:** Create epic combos where one merge triggers another, clearing the board and racking up massive scores.
- **Hint System:** Stuck? Toggle the **?** button to see valid moves highlighted with a neon-ring target.

---

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Grab & Drag:** Take the active tile from the queue on the right.
2. **Scan the Grid:** Look for neighbors that can be divided (e.g., a **12** next to a **3**).
3. **Drop to Merge:** Place your tile! If it divides a neighbor, they'll merge into the quotient (12 / 3 = **4**).
4. **Clear the Board:** If the result is **1**, both tiles vanish! Keep the grid from filling up to stay in the game.
